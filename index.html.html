The Challenge

Registration is Mandatory. The Process is Complex.

Act 48 of 2000 requires all persons practising in construction management to be registered. The penalty is a fine equal to double the remuneration received.

01

Terminology Gap

SACPCMP requires specific language that differs from your company titles. Your experience is valid — but it must be translated into SACPCMP terminology.

02

Documentation Burden

Prescribed CV, project profiles, two 1,500-word reports under 9 PMBOK knowledge areas, CPD records — significant and format-specific requirements.

03

Route Confusion

Five registration routes, NQF levels, credit counts, accreditation — most candidates are unsure which path applies to them.

About Us

The Construction Management Foundation

A non-profit company dedicated to advancing construction management professionalism across Africa through accessible registration support, skills development, and industry transformation.

Our Mission

To professionalise construction management in Africa by removing barriers to SACPCMP registration and building a pipeline of registered professionals who deliver safer, higher-quality construction outcomes.

Our Vision

A construction industry where every practising construction project manager and construction manager is professionally registered, competent, and committed to ethical practice — regardless of their socioeconomic background.

Our Values

Accessibility — Professional registration should not be a privilege of the few. We make the process navigable for all.

Excellence — We hold ourselves and our candidates to the highest professional standards.

Transformation — We actively support historically disadvantaged professionals in achieving registration.

Integrity — We operate transparently and ethically in everything we do.

Impact — We measure success by registrations achieved, not services sold.

How It Works

The 5-Phase Registration Journey

Each phase has defined deliverables, timelines, and quality checkpoints. Total programme duration: 12–16 weeks from engagement to interview readiness.

1
1–2 wks

Initial Assessment & Pre-Screening

We start with a thorough assessment of your qualifications, experience, and career profile against SACPCMP requirements.

Activities: Pre-screening form completion, CV review, qualification verification, registration route analysis (Routes 1–5), Pr. CPM vs Pr. CM recommendation, programme proposal and fee quotation.

Deliverables: Eligibility assessment, route recommendation, programme proposal.

2
1–2 wks

Comprehensive Gap Analysis

A deep-dive assessment producing a multi-tab workbook that maps your entire profile against every SACPCMP requirement.

Activities: CV assessment against 11+ SACPCMP sections, qualification mapping against 5 routes, experience mapping against 6 project stages and 9 PMBOK knowledge areas, RAG-rated readiness scoring, terminology gap identification, personalised action plan with milestones.

Deliverables: 6-tab gap analysis Excel workbook, formal consultation meeting.

3
4–6 wks

Documentation Preparation

The most intensive phase. We prepare your complete application documentation to SACPCMP standards — not templates, but your actual finished documents.

Activities: CV rewrite in SACPCMP prescribed format, organogram creation, Annexure A1 project profiles (min 2), Annexure A2 Successes report (~1,500 words), Annexure A2 Challenges report (~1,500 words), CPD record compilation (Categories A/B/C), cover letter, document consistency review, terminology alignment check.

Deliverables: CV, A1 profiles, A2 reports (x2), CPD record, cover letter, consistency verification.

4
2–4 wks

Application Submission & Monitoring

We guide you through the SACPCMP portal, support document uploads, and then actively monitor your application through each assessment phase.

Activities: Portal guidance, final document review, upload support, application confirmation, fortnightly progress reviews, SACPCMP liaison, response preparation for additional information requests.

Deliverables: Submission confirmation, Gantt schedule, meeting tracker, document tracker.

5
3–4 wks

Interview Preparation & Post-Registration

Structured preparation for the SACPCMP professional interview — the final hurdle. We prepare you to demonstrate competence with confidence.

Activities: 9 knowledge area coaching with project-specific examples, SA legislation review (OHS Act, Construction Regs, contracts), SACPCMP IDOW study, 200-question preparation bank, mock interview session, post-registration CPD planning.

Deliverables: Interview prep guide, mock interview recording, legislation notes, CPD plan, completion certificate.
